TARAZ

Taraz is a city and the administrative center of Jambyl Region in Kazakhstan, located on the Talas (Taraz) River in the south of the country near the border with Kyrgyzstan. It had a population of 330,100

Taraz is very popular for the variety of great food it has. Some of the top food items include:

  1. Doner
  2. Chicken
  3. Burgers and Sandwiches
  4. shawarma
  5. Desserts

While the view in the city is beautiful, the sounds are not as lovely.
